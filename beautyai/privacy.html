Article One

Information We Collect · 我們收集的資料

Account Information · 帳戶資料

If you create an account or sign in, we may collect your email address, user ID, display name, avatar, Beauty Points balance, purchase records, reward records, and app usage records needed to provide account, purchase, and history features.

如您建立帳戶或登入，我們可能會收集您的電郵地址、用戶 ID、顯示名稱、頭像、Beauty Points 餘額、購買紀錄、獎勵紀錄，以及提供帳戶、購買和歷史紀錄功能所需的使用紀錄。

Image Information · 圖像資訊

When you choose to use BeautyAI's AI analysis or styling simulation features, you understand and agree that the photo you select may be uploaded to our server for processing before the analysis result or processed image is returned to you.

當您選擇使用 BeautyAI 的 AI 分析或造型模擬功能時，您理解並同意，您所選擇的相片可能會上傳至我們的伺服器進行處理，然後將分析結果或處理後的圖片傳回給您。

Image Information may include the photo you choose and generated Before/After styling images. BeautyAI does not intentionally collect or store EXIF metadata from photos, such as GPS location, device model, ISO, camera information, or creation time. Photos may be compressed or re-encoded before processing, which may remove metadata from the image file.

圖像資訊可能包括您所選擇的相片，以及生成的 Before/After 造型圖片。BeautyAI 不會有意收集或儲存相片中的 EXIF 元資料，例如 GPS 位置、裝置型號、ISO、相機資訊或建立時間。相片在處理前可能會被壓縮或重新編碼，這可能會移除圖片檔案中的元資料。

We use Image Information only to provide the AI analysis and styling simulation requested by you.

我們只會使用圖像資訊，以提供您所要求的 AI 分析和造型模擬。

Face Data & Facial Feature Information · Face Data 及面部特徵資訊

For the purpose of this policy, "Face Data" means biometric or face-recognition data derived from a face image, such as Face ID data, TrueDepth data, facial mapping information, facial landmarks, faceprints, face embeddings, biometric templates, or facial recognition identifiers used to identify or authenticate a specific person.

在本政策中，「Face Data」指從人臉圖片衍生、用於生物識別或人臉識別的資料，例如 Face ID 資料、TrueDepth 資料、面部映射資料、面部關鍵點、人臉指紋、面部特徵向量、生物識別模板，或可用於識別或驗證特定人士身份的人臉識別識別碼。

BeautyAI does not collect, store, or retain Face Data such as Face ID data, TrueDepth data, facial mapping information, facial landmarks, faceprints, face embeddings, biometric templates, or facial recognition identifiers used to identify or authenticate a specific person. BeautyAI does not use Face Data to identify, verify, authenticate, track, or recognize a specific person.

BeautyAI 不會收集、儲存或保留 Face Data，例如 Face ID 資料、TrueDepth 資料、面部映射資料、面部關鍵點、人臉指紋、面部特徵向量、生物識別模板，或可用於識別或驗證特定人士身份的人臉識別識別碼。BeautyAI 不會使用 Face Data 來識別、驗證、認證、追蹤或辨認特定人士。

BeautyAI may temporarily detect facial areas or facial features during AI processing only to provide the analysis or styling simulation requested by you, such as face shape reference, skin observations, makeup suitability, hairstyle direction, and Before/After styling effects. Such temporary facial processing information is not stored as a separate face-recognition file, face template, faceprint, or biometric identifier.

BeautyAI 可能會在 AI 處理期間暫時偵測面部區域或面部特徵，只為提供您所要求的分析或造型模擬，例如臉型參考、膚況觀察、妝容適配、髮型方向及 Before/After 造型效果。此類暫時性面部處理資訊不會被儲存為獨立的人臉識別檔案、人臉模板、人臉指紋或生物識別識別碼。

Facial feature information used only for temporary facial detection or feature processing is not retained by BeautyAI after the requested processing is completed.

在完成您所要求的處理後，BeautyAI 不會保留僅為暫時性面部偵測或特徵處理而使用的面部特徵資訊。

BeautyAI does not share Face Data with third parties for any purpose, no matter for storage, facial recognition, identity verification, advertising, tracking, or AI model training. When BeautyAI uses third-party AI service providers, we share only the Image Information selected by the user and the related API input/output content needed to provide the requested analysis or styling simulation. BeautyAI does not ask nor permit these third-party service providers to create, store, or retain Face Data, such as Face ID data, TrueDepth data, facial mapping information, facial landmarks, faceprints, face embeddings, biometric templates, or facial recognition identifiers used to identify or authenticate a specific person. To the extent that third-party service providers temporarily retain API inputs, outputs, or service logs, such retention applies to Image Information and API processing records, not Face Data as defined in this policy. For more information about how third-party services process Image Information and API input/output records, including their processing purposes and retention periods, please refer to Section 4, "Third-Party Services," of this Privacy Policy.

BeautyAI 不會將 Face Data 分享予第三方作儲存、人臉識別、身份驗證、廣告、追蹤或 AI 模型訓練用途。當 BeautyAI 使用第三方 AI 服務供應商時，我們只會分享用戶所選擇的圖像資訊，以及提供用戶所要求的分析或造型模擬所需的相關 API 輸入／輸出內容。BeautyAI 不會要求該等第三方服務供應商建立、儲存或保留 Face Data，例如 Face ID 資料、TrueDepth 資料、面部映射資料、面部關鍵點、人臉指紋、面部特徵向量、生物識別模板，或可用於識別或驗證特定人士身份的人臉識別識別碼。如第三方服務供應商基於其服務條款暫時保留 API 輸入、輸出或服務紀錄，該等保存適用於圖像資訊及 API 處理紀錄，而非本政策所定義的 Face Data。有關第三方服務如何處理圖像資訊及 API 輸入／輸出紀錄，包括其處理目的及保存期限，請參閱本私隱政策第 4 點「第三方服務」。

Article Two

Analysis Results & History · 分析結果及歷史紀錄

BeautyAI may generate non-biometric analysis results, such as style suggestions, face shape references, skin observations, makeup direction, hairstyle suggestions, scores, radar values, and Before/After styling records.

BeautyAI 可能會生成非生物識別性質的分析結果，例如造型建議、臉型參考、膚況觀察、妝容方向、髮型建議、分數、雷達數值及 Before/After 造型紀錄。

If you are signed in, these results may be saved in your private account history so that you can review previous analysis and styling simulation records. These results are used only to provide your personal history and app functionality. They are not used for facial recognition, identity verification, advertising targeting, or tracking.

如您已登入，這些結果可能會儲存在您的私人帳戶歷史紀錄中，讓您查看過往分析及造型模擬紀錄。這些結果只會用於提供您的個人歷史紀錄及 App 功能，不會用於人臉識別、身份驗證、廣告定向或追蹤。

If you delete a history record or delete your account, the related saved records will be deleted from active systems, unless limited retention is required for legal, security, fraud-prevention, payment, or compliance purposes.

如您刪除歷史紀錄或刪除帳戶，相關已儲存紀錄將從現行系統刪除，除非因法律、安全、防詐騙、付款或合規原因需要有限度保留。

Article Four

Third-Party Services · 第三方服務

BeautyAI shares information only with service providers needed to provide the app features, process payments, operate secure storage, verify rewards, or maintain service reliability.

BeautyAI 只會向提供 App 功能、付款處理、安全儲存、獎勵驗證或維持服務穩定性所需的服務供應商分享資料。

Google Vertex AI / Gemini

We use Google Vertex AI / Gemini to process selected photos for AI analysis and primary styling generation. We share the selected Image Information with Google only to generate the analysis or styling result requested by you.

我們使用 Google Vertex AI / Gemini 處理所選相片，以提供 AI 分析及主要造型生成功能。我們只會為生成您所要求的分析或造型結果，而向 Google 分享所選圖像資訊。

For Google Vertex AI / Gemini on Google Cloud, Google may cache customer data, including inputs and outputs, in memory for service performance with a 24-hour TTL. If BeautyAI uses billing-enabled Gemini API logs, supported API logs expire after 55 days by default. BeautyAI does not opt in to share API logs or datasets with Google for model training or product improvement.

就 Google Cloud 上的 Google Vertex AI / Gemini 而言，Google 可能會為提升服務效能，將客戶資料，包括輸入及輸出，暫存在記憶體中，TTL 為 24 小時。如 BeautyAI 使用已啟用 billing 的 Gemini API logs，受支援的 API logs 預設會在 55 日後失效。BeautyAI 不會選擇將 API logs 或 datasets 分享予 Google 作模型訓練或產品改善用途。

OpenAI Image API

If the primary generation service fails, BeautyAI may use the OpenAI Image API as a fallback for styling generation. We share selected Image Information with OpenAI only to generate the styling result requested by you.

如主要生成服務失敗，BeautyAI 可能會使用 OpenAI Image API 作為造型生成的後備服務。我們只會為生成您所要求的造型結果，而向 OpenAI 分享所選圖像資訊。

OpenAI may retain API inputs and outputs in abuse monitoring logs for up to 30 days by default, unless longer retention is required by law or is reasonably necessary to protect OpenAI's services or third parties from harm. BeautyAI does not opt in to share API logs or datasets with OpenAI for model training or product improvement.

OpenAI 預設可能會在 abuse monitoring logs 中保留 API 輸入及輸出最多 30 日，除非法律要求較長保存期，或為保護 OpenAI 服務或第三方免受損害而合理需要較長保存期。BeautyAI 不會選擇將 API logs 或 datasets 分享予 OpenAI 作模型訓練或產品改善用途。

Supabase

We use Supabase for account authentication, secure database storage, private account history, and storage infrastructure. If you are signed in, your account records, analysis results, generated images, and private history records may be stored in Supabase so that you can access your records in the app.

我們使用 Supabase 作帳戶驗證、安全資料庫儲存、私人帳戶歷史紀錄及儲存基建。如您已登入，您的帳戶紀錄、分析結果、生成圖片及私人歷史紀錄可能會儲存在 Supabase，以便您在 App 內查看自己的紀錄。

Supabase acts as our infrastructure provider and does not use your information for advertising, tracking, facial recognition, or AI model training.

Supabase 作為我們的基建服務供應商，不會將您的資料用於廣告、追蹤、人臉識別或 AI 模型訓練。

Apple App Store

We use Apple App Store services to process in-app purchases. Apple may process payment-related information under Apple's own privacy policy. BeautyAI does not receive your full payment card information.

我們使用 Apple App Store 服務處理 App 內購買。Apple 可能會根據其私隱政策處理付款相關資料。BeautyAI 不會收到您的完整付款卡資料。

Google AdMob

We may use Google AdMob to display ads and verify rewarded-ad events. AdMob may process advertising identifiers, device information, and ad interaction data according to Google's applicable advertising and privacy policies. BeautyAI does not share your Image Information or Facial Feature Information with AdMob for advertising targeting.

我們可能會使用 Google AdMob 顯示廣告及驗證獎勵廣告事件。AdMob 可能會根據 Google 適用的廣告及私隱政策處理廣告識別碼、裝置資訊及廣告互動資料。BeautyAI 不會將您的圖像資訊或面部特徵資訊分享予 AdMob 作廣告定向用途。

Article Five

Data Retention · 資料保存

BeautyAI does not retain Face Data such as Face ID data, TrueDepth data, facial mapping information, facial landmarks, faceprints, face embeddings, biometric templates, or facial recognition identifiers used to identify or authenticate a specific person.

BeautyAI 不會保留生物識別 Face Data，例如 Face ID 資料、TrueDepth 資料、面部映射資料、面部關鍵點、人臉指紋、面部特徵向量、生物識別模板，或可用於識別或驗證特定人士身份的人臉識別識別碼。

Facial feature information used only for temporary facial detection or feature processing is not retained by BeautyAI after the requested processing is completed.

在完成您所要求的處理後，BeautyAI 不會保留僅為暫時性面部偵測或特徵處理而使用的面部特徵資訊。

Image Information and analysis results saved in your private account history are retained so that you can view previous analysis and Before/After records. You can delete saved records at any time in the app. If you delete a history record or delete your account, the related saved history images and analysis records will be deleted from active systems, unless limited retention is required for legal, security, fraud-prevention, payment, or compliance purposes.

儲存在您私人帳戶歷史紀錄中的圖像資訊及分析結果，會被保留以便您查看過往分析及 Before/After 紀錄。您可以隨時在 App 內刪除已儲存紀錄。如您刪除歷史紀錄或刪除帳戶，相關已儲存的歷史圖片及分析紀錄將從現行系統刪除，除非因法律、安全、防詐騙、付款或合規原因需要有限度保留。

我們採取合理的技術及組織措施保護您的資料，包括加密傳輸及存取控制。然而，任何傳輸或儲存方式均不能保證百分百安全。

AI 分析及造型模擬屬於自願使用的功能。如您不希望 BeautyAI 為 AI 分析或造型模擬處理您所選擇的相片，您可以選擇不使用該等功能。您亦可以隨時刪除相關已儲存紀錄。
